Alappuzha:Commercial Capital of Kerala


Alappuzha (or Alleppey) is a city on the Laccadive Sea in the southern Indian state of Kerala. It's best known for houseboat cruises along the rustic Kerala backwaters, a network of tranquil canals and lagoons.


Places To Visit


Alleppey Backwaters


Take a trip with a house boat through the lovely backwaters of Kerala and wind down and relax.

Ambalappuzha Krishna Temple


An amazing temple with divine prasadam of special payasam, which is in great demand.

Alappuzha Beach

Alappuzha Beach has an old pier which extend to sea is over 150 years old.
