Places To Visit


Edakkal Caves


Edakkal caves are well known for its pictorial depictions (cave art), which are thought to be of 6000 B C. To achieve the hollows, one needs to trek through the Ambukutty Mala.

Neelimala View Point


A place for thrill seekers, Neelimala View point can be reached by means of trekking at the same time offering a few astonishing trek trails.

Bamboo Forest

The Nilgiri Biosphere Region additionally reaches out to an extensive extent of teak timberlands, bamboo trenches and overwhelms. A perfect safari area, deer, buffalo, cheetah and wild bears are among the spotted creatures here.
