Pathanamthitta:Pilgrim Capital of Kerala


The district is close to the western Ghats and is endowed with scenic landscape comprising of low undulating hills, extensive stretches of lush forests, rivers and charming countryside. It is also known for exuberant fairs and festivals.


Places To Visit


Perenthenaruvi Waterfall


A natural waterfall with captivating surroundings is situated on the banks of the River Pamba.

Gavi


Gavi is situated some 14 km away from the famed Periyar Tiger Reserve and is heaven on Earth,literally

Konni Forest Reserve

This place is considered to be the equivalent to an elephant's cage.Elephants are trained here.


Popular Things To Do



Adavi Eco-Tourism

This is a nice destination mainly for family trips.The main attraction is bowl boat riding through backwaters.

Shopping in Pathanamthitta

If you are visiting this wonderful town, make sure buy its specialty such as Aramula Kannadi.

Explore Periyar National Park

The park is a repository of rare, endemic and endangered flora and fauna.
